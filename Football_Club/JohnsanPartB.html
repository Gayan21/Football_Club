DESCRIPTION
Paris Saint German is a soccer club based in Paris, France. It was founded in 12th August,
1970 after the merger of Paris Football Club and Stade Saint-German. PSG plays in the France Ligue 1.
This club is well known for their record of winning trophies and famous players recruitments
like Zlatan Ibrahimovic, Thiago Silva, Edinson Cavani, Neymar and Mbappe.
